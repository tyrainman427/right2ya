Onboarding Steps - Accepting Jobs

Step 1: Accepting Jobs

In this step, you will learn how to accept delivery jobs:

  1. Open the app and log in to your driver account.
  2. Check the available jobs in your area.
  3. Review the job details, including pickup and drop-off locations.
  4. Accept the job if you are available and willing to complete it.
  5. Follow the provided directions to the pickup location.
  6. Complete the delivery and mark the job as finished in the app.
