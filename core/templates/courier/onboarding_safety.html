Step 2: Safety and Concerns

Your safety and the safety of others is a top priority. Here are some important guidelines:

  • Follow all traffic laws and regulations.
  • Use your seatbelt at all times.
  • Do not use your mobile device while driving.
  • Be aware of your surroundings and drive defensively.
  • If you encounter any issues or concerns during a delivery, contact our support team immediately.

If you have any questions or need assistance, please don't hesitate to reach out.
